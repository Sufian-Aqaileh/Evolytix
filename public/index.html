Platform overview

Built around the real needs of accounting work.

Evolytix is not meant to be a generic assistant. The platform is organized around major accounting tasks so users can choose the kind of analysis they need and move through a more focused workflow.

Auditing

Review financial records, detect anomalies, and support internal and external audit workflows.

Financial Analysis

Interpret statements, surface trends, and help firms understand performance with faster insight.

Tax Consultation

Support tax-focused decisions with organized, task-specific analysis for accounting teams.
